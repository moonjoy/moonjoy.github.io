About

I build experiences by breaking down difficult problems into intuitive, empathy-driven, and simplified solutions for end-to-end products. My work spans UX research, interaction design, visual systems, and front-end implementation, so ideas can move from concept to shipped experiences with consistency and clarity. I care deeply about accessibility, usability, and thoughtful details that make products feel effortless for real people.
